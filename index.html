Home Brew

Home Brew is an E-Commerce website that sells third-wave coffee products. The platform efficiently manages tasks such as adding and removing items from the cart, overseeing cart state, and integrates with Stripe for purchase transactions. However, please note that since Home Brew is a simulated store and not operational, the backend server has been disabled, rendering Stripe inactive at the moment.

LyricsLink.

LyricsLink serves as a comprehensive dashboard, simplifying the process of song planning for individuals utilizing Planning Center accounts. This platform empowers users to seamlessly create and oversee events, effortlessly importing songs from their Planning Center organization. At the core of its functionality is the unique capability to take events live, producing shareable links for instantaneous access to song lyrics by anyone with the link. This streamlined approach significantly accelerates the setup process for lyrics, ensuring prompt formatting and easy sharing. Check out a brief walkthrough here.

  • React.js
  • Express.js
  • Postgres DB
  • OAuth 2.0

Fantasy Basketball Discord Bot

This discord bot is designed to provide information about today's NBA games upon user prompt. Users can interact with the bot by triggering a specific command, and in response, the bot retrieves the relevant information about NBA games scheduled for the current day. The retrieved details are then presented as a message within the Discord channel, offering users a convenient and quick way to stay updated on the day's NBA matchups.

  • Express.js
  • Discord.js
